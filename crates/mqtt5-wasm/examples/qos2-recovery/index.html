MQTT QoS 2 Mid-Flight Recovery Demo

Demonstrates QoS 2 exactly-once delivery surviving a connection interruption mid-handshake. The broker persists in-flight state and resumes the QoS 2 flow on reconnect.

QoS 2 Flow: PUBLISH → PUBREC → PUBREL → PUBCOMP. When the subscriber disconnects after receiving PUBLISH but before PUBREC completes, the broker retains the in-flight message and resends it (with DUP=1) on reconnect.
